College Study Tips

Why are study tips important?

  1. Time Management
  2. Organization
  3. Transferable Skills

Here are my favorite study tips:

  1. Note a manageable To-Do list. Don't overstress yourself with work.
  2. Take on one subject at a time
  3. Give yourself some short study breaks

Remember to always set time asside to study, don't try to commit to just 15 minutes every day. Stay hydrated and make sure to keep your study area organized, to avoid confusion. Tak everything one step at a time.
